== Description ==

Bosa Mobile App is multipurpose tech business theme. Bosa Mobile App is beautiful, fast, lightweight, responsive, extremely customizable theme that you can use as a foundation to build versatile sites such as  agency, app, application, clean, download, mobile, modern, showcase, social media, startup and preferably ideal for any type of app sites. Bosa Mobile App is a child theme of Bosa, a free multipurpose WordPress theme. Bosa Mobile App works perfectly with Gutenberg and the most popular page builder Elementor that can easily drag-and-drop your ideas from the interface. Bosa Mobile App is built with SEO, speed, and usability in mind with the multiple Header & Footer layouts, predesigned starter sites includes awesome Home & Inner Pages that is incredibly customizable and powerful enough to take a stand among the crowd. Bosa Mobile App is compatible with all major plugins like WooCommerce, Yoast, Contact form 7, Mailchimp for WordPress, bbPress, etc. Looking for a Multipurpose app theme? Look no further! Browse the demo to see that it's the only theme you will ever need: https://demo.bosathemes.com/bosa/mobile-app/
